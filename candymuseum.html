MUSEUM OF CANDY

The transistor is a two junction, three terminal semiconductor device which has three regions namely the emitter region, the base region, and the collector region. There are two types of transistors. A NPN transistor has an n types emitter, a p types base and an n types collector while a PNP transistor has a p type emitter, an n type base and a p types collector. The emitter is heavily doped, base region is thin and lightly doped and collector is moderately doped and is the largest.
